Who is Edmond Kombat

A dynamic Ghanaian leader, youth advocate, and visionary committed to building a just, inclusive, and progressive society

Edmond B. Kombat is a dynamic Ghanaian leader whose career bridges energy management, law, and sustainable agribusiness. He currently serves as the Managing Director of Tema Oil Refinery (TOR), where he leads a new wave of reforms aimed at reviving Ghana's premier refinery through efficiency, investment, and innovation.

Education & Academic Excellence

Edmond holds a Bachelor's degree in Political Science and History from the University of Ghana, where he once served as SRC President; an LL.B from the GIMPA Faculty of Law; and a Qualifying Certificate (BL) from the Ghana School of Law.

He also earned a Master of Public Administration (MPA) from the Harvard Kennedy School, where he was an Edward S. Mason Fellow, further strengthening his expertise in public policy and leadership.

Energy Sector Leadership

A seasoned professional with both private and public sector experience, Edmond has held key positions within TOR, the Ministry of Energy, and BOST, and has served as a co-founder of the Institute for Energy Security (IES)‚Äîa leading think tank focused on energy research and policy advocacy in Ghana.

His professional portfolio extends into energy strategy, financial management, and law, and includes experience with Bank of America Merrill Lynch and the Ghana Stock Exchange, where he is a licensed stockbroker.

Beyond Public Service

Beyond his contributions to the energy sector, Edmond is the Founder and Chairman of Edkom Group and Chief Executive Officer of Oyster Agribusiness, an award-winning enterprise promoting climate-smart agriculture and empowering thousands of smallholder farmers.

Through Oyster Agribusiness, he has spearheaded sustainable farming initiatives that have impacted over 4,500 farmers, cultivated more than 20,000 acres, and generated over GH¬¢60 million in income for rural producers.

Outside public life, Edmond enjoys mentoring young people, engaging in community projects, and contributing to discussions on sustainable development and social innovation. He is passionate about Ghana's future and believes in empowering the next generation to lead with courage and conscience.

Leadership Philosophy

Edmond believes leadership is about vision, empathy, and service. He continues to champion causes that strengthen institutions, empower communities, and promote accountability in governance.
