About Edmond B. Kombat

Edmond B. Kombat is a Ghanaian leader, lawyer, and entrepreneur currently serving as Managing Director of Tema Oil Refinery (TOR). Known for his bold reform-driven leadership, Edmond blends experience from the energy, legal, and agribusiness sectors to drive sustainable transformation.

As Founder and CEO of Oyster Agribusiness, he has empowered thousands of farmers through climate-smart agriculture. A former SRC President at the University of Ghana and Edward S. Mason Fellow at Harvard Kennedy School, Edmond represents a new generation of leaders dedicated to innovation, integrity, and national development.
